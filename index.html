ABOUT US

Small studio. Weird ideas. Serious games.

BETESHIG studios is an indie game studio driven by a simple belief: great games come from spite, snacks, and an unhealthy attachment to pixel art. We've spent time honing our skills and building our voice — and now we're respawning as a real studio.

We build with Godot, create art in Aseprite, and craft 3D assets in Blender.

Engineering — Adi wrangles code, fights physics engines, and somehow turns spaghetti into shipping software. If it runs, he built it. If it crashes, he's already fixing it.

Art Direction — Mircea turns vague ideas into pixels that slap. From character sprites to full environments, if it looks good, it's because he wouldn't let it not.

5 games, 5 genres, zero sensible ideas. We've been shipping since 2024 and we're not slowing down — every project starts as a wild "what if" and somehow ends up on itch.io before anyone can stop us.
